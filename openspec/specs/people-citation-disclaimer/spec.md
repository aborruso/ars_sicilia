## Requirements
### Requirement: People list disclaimer
The video page MUST display an AI disclaimer beneath the “Persone citate” heading when the people list is present.

#### Scenario: Disclaimer appears with people list
- **WHEN** a video has extracted people listed
- **THEN** a warning box appears directly below the “Persone citate” heading
- **AND** the warning states the list is AI-extracted and may contain errors in names and roles
